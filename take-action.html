TAKE ACTION

This doesn't have to be the end for our planet. Keep reading to find out how you can put an end to the plastic waste that is harming our home.

Learn More

If you would like to learn more about the issue, here is a list of websites that can provide some knowledge!


  • The United States Environmental Program: Our Planet is Choking On Plastic. A knowledgable article on the causes behind plastic waste and what it does to the Earth.
  • National Geographic: The World's Plastic Pollution Problem Explained. National Geographic explains just why this issue is so serious. "Every year, about 8 million tons of plastic waste escapes into the oceans from coastal nations."
  • The Alliance to End Plastic Waste. This site pledges efforts to end plastic waste, explains their actions to do so, and how one can get involved..

Get Involved

There are various ways to get involved. For one, you can directly limit plastic waste by involving yourself in road or beach clean ups, such as the Ocean Cleanup. Additionally, you can support businesses that are taking initiative to end plastic waste. This may include food chains that have switched to paper straws, and so on. Learn more about the initiative at Covestro's vebsite.


Your action today can be to call your congress person demanding change. This change may include requiring plastic companies to responsibly recycle, enact single-use plastic bans, etc. Read more and help Greenpeace's initiatives here: GreenPeace: Tell Congress to Tackle the Single-use Plastic Crisis.
